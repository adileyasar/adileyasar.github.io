About Me

Hello there! I am a doctoral researcher working on experiments to understand human cooperation and cumulative cultural evolution.

Research Interests

  • Which contexts enable human cooperation? I study this question by manipulating experimental contexts using pre-defined bots in a public goods game.
  • Which solutions constrain human innovation? In a world increasingly influenced by Artificial Intelligence, I am particularly interested in understanding how AI-generated solutions may anchor human problem-solving, potentially limiting exploration and material technological advancement.
