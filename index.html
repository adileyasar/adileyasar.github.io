Adile Yasar (M.Sc.)

Hello there! I am a doctoral researcher at the Max Planck Institute for Evolutionary Biology. I use experimental approaches to understand human cooperation and cumulative cultural evolution.

About Me

The key questions I am interested in are the following:

  • Which contexts enable human cooperation?: I study this question by manipulating experimental contexts using pre-defined bots in a public goods game.

  • Which solutions constrain human innovation? In a world increasingly influenced by Artificial Intelligence, I am interested in understanding how AI-generated solutions may anchor human problem-solving, potentially limiting exploration and technological advancement.
